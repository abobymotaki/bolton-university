Western International College (WINC) is the non-academic infrastructure provider of the University of Bolton and its academic center in Ras Al Khaimah. Western International College and University of Bolton, Academic Center - Ras Al Khaimah are all part of the same institution that has been operating in Ras Al Khaimah since 2008. The University of Bolton, Academic Center - Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates is part of the International Knowledgucate. A group of institutions based in the United Arab Emirates. Knowledgucate owns and operates a chain of play schools, K-12 schools, pre-university schools, undergraduate and graduate institutions and universities in India. In addition to offering online programs, it also owns and operates educational institutions in the UAE and the UK.

University of Bolton, Academic Center - Ras Al Khaimah was first established in 2008 and is licensed by the Government of Ras Al Khaimah. We offer undergraduate and graduate programs in a variety of fields.

The academic center specializes in areas such as; Accounting, Construction Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Technology, Business Administration, Law, Psychology and Computer Science. Students from various academic backgrounds and levels can join the University of Bolton's Academic Center to obtain their undergraduate and graduate degrees. The Academic Center is housed in a purpose-built facility, which is just part of our commitment to providing the best possible experience for students. All academic programs are based on this campus with the foundation programs we offer as approved entry to programs at the University of Bolton.

The University of Bolton, Academic Center - Ras Al Khaimah is the first institution to apply for and receive a license from RAKEZ, which has a newly established licensing system that includes a comprehensive quality assurance process that all academic institutions in the emirate. now get down to business. must work to fulfill it. Western International College (WINC) was proud to be the first institution to receive this license.

The academic center operates in a 42,000 square meter building with classrooms, lecture halls, office space, a library and engineering and computing laboratories. In addition, our students have access to a wide range of learning and external facilities on campus through a long-standing and active student council. There are also student apartments on site that offer high quality accommodation and services at a reasonable price.
